About

There are many different illness’ in this world, both physical and mental. Depression is one of them, it is both mental and physical to the human body. One can feel empty, lonely, lifeless or numb to feeling. There is no desire to activities or life in general. There are many symptoms to depression a well such as; loss of interest, hopelessness, no energy, weight gain/loss and many more. Another symptom is also self harm, depression can make someone so disoriented in life to the point where someone could hurt themselves to either cope with the disease or to simply hurt themselves. Every 1 in 10 Canadians will experience depression, that’s 10% of the population who have experienced depression. It’s a very serious mental disease that a lot of people deal with today.

Our goal is to make as much people possible aware of this disease and how it truly affects people everyday. We want to people to gain knowledge and the understanding of what this word “Depression” really means. How many people it affects, what it does to them, why it does it to them. We’re also here to help people who suffer from it or may know someone who suffers from depression. We have the answers on why they may do it and how you can go about helping them. We’re here for the people who think there is no use in living life and feel numbness of feeling. You’re not alone, we’re here for you and we can help.
